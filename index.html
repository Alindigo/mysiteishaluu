For U 💖

She was pretty, she was gold 💛
I knew it, and so did the world 🌎
Everyone wanted to take her, to hold,
But I had no need for a prize to be earned.

I never wished to claim or own,
Never to dim her shining light ✨
I only dreamed to build a home,
Where she’d be safe, where things felt right.

A place where love ❤️ is not a trade,
Where kindness comes with no disguise.
But the world is cold, and hearts will fade—
Yet here I stand, with open eyes.

Need to talk? I’m here, just say. Need to cry? I’ll stay, don’t hide. When life gets rough, I’ll clear the way, With open arms, right by your side.🎀💖
